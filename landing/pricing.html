Frequently asked questions

What happens after the 7-day trial?
Your subscription continues at $4.99/month (Conversation Pro) or $7.99/month (Finance). You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and won't be charged. The open-source engine is always free.
What's the difference between free and paid?
The open-source engine runs locally and includes the core 17-channel analysis. Paid plans add hosted API access (no setup), real-time steering, longitudinal tracking, and the Finance plan adds the full risk overlay with exposure scalar.
What does the finance overlay actually do?
It takes OHLCV candle data and measures structural disorder in price action using the same 17-channel engine. It outputs an exposure scalar (0.0–1.0) that reduces position sizing during structural instability. 100% bounded regime accuracy (126/126 graded).
Is this financial advice?
No. ReNoUn is a structural observability tool, not a trading signal. It measures disorder, not direction. It's designed as a risk overlay to pair with your existing strategy. Always do your own research.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account. No contracts, no commitments.
